About

One attorney, accountable by name

Nordfara is the immigration practice of Patrick Smith, Attorney at Law — built deliberately small, so that judgment, drafting, and client communication come from the same person.

The attorney

Patrick Smith

Patrick Smith is a Texas-licensed attorney (Texas Bar No. 24106451) practicing US immigration law — a federal practice that allows representation of clients throughout the United States and abroad. His work spans the two halves of Nordfara: employment-based immigration for companies moving talent into the American market, and family-based immigration for Spanish-speaking clients across Texas and the border region.

He works personally in English and Spanish. Client communication is a professional commitment, not a slogan: matters are explained in plain language, in the client's language, by the attorney handling the case.

The firm's European practice grows from the same conviction that borders should be navigable with honest guidance — for European employers entering the US, and for Americans making the journey the other way.

Norðfara

Why the name

The Old Norse norðfara — north journey — belonged to the word family the Norse used for those who emigrated. A Spanish-speaking client hears el norte; a European client hears something of home. The name is a commitment to both: the same journey, guided with the same care.
