Norðfara

A name with a heading

In Old Norse, norðr is north and fara is to journey — the word family the Norse used for their emigrants. Our Spanish-speaking clients hear el norte. Our European clients hear home. Every client is on the same journey: toward a working future in a new country, with someone accountable holding the map.
